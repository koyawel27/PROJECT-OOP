WELS Cycling Warranty Policy

We stand behind our products with industry-leading warranty coverage

Bicycle Frame Warranty

All WELS branded bicycle frames are covered by our comprehensive warranty:

  • Carbon Frames: Limited lifetime warranty for the original owner
  • Aluminum Frames: 5-year warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Suspension Components: 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Paint & Finish: 1-year warranty against defects in materials and workmanship

Our warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship when used normally according to our guidelines.

Components & Accessories

Components and accessories carry their own specific warranty terms:

  • WELS Branded Components: 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Third-party Components: Covered by the original manufacturer's warranty
  • Electronic Components: 1-year warranty against defects in materials and workmanship
  • Apparel & Soft Goods: 90-day warranty against manufacturing defects

Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

What's Not Covered

Our warranty does not cover damages resulting from:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Improper assembly or maintenance
  • Accidents, crashes, or misuse
  • Modifications to the original frame or components
  • Commercial use or rental applications
  • Damage from extreme or improper riding conditions
  • Failure to perform regular maintenance

WELS reserves the right to determine whether a product is eligible for warranty coverage.

How to Submit a Claim

If you believe your product is eligible for warranty service:

  1. Contact our Customer Service team through our Contact Us page
  2. Provide your order number, purchase date, and photos/description of the issue
  3. Our technical team will evaluate your claim and provide instructions
  4. If approved, you'll receive either a repair, replacement, or store credit

Most warranty claims are processed within 5-7 business days.

Need Help With a Warranty Claim?

Our customer service team is ready to assist you with any warranty questions or claims.
