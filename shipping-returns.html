Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

At WELS Cycling, we strive to provide fast, reliable shipping options to all our customers across the Philippines. Below are the details of our shipping services:

Shipping Timeframes

Location Standard Shipping Express Shipping
Metro Manila 2-3 business days Next business day
Luzon (outside Metro Manila) 3-5 business days 2 business days
Visayas 5-7 business days 3 business days
Mindanao 7-10 business days 3-5 business days

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the size and weight of the items in your order and your delivery location:

  • Accessories and apparel: ₱150 - ₱500 depending on weight and location
  • Bikes: ₱1,000 - ₱2,500 depending on model and location
  • FREE shipping on orders over ₱5,000 (Standard shipping only)

Note: Shipping times may be affected by weather conditions, holidays, or other unforeseen circumstances. We'll keep you updated on any delays.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, we offer a simple returns and exchanges process.

Return Policy

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging with tags attached.
  • Bikes can be returned within 14 days if unridden and in pristine condition.
  • Sale items are eligible for returns within 14 days.
  • Customized items cannot be returned unless they are defective.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team via email at returns@welscycling.com or call +63 (2) 8123 4567.
  2. Fill out the return form that came with your order, or download it from our website.
  3. Package the item(s) securely in the original packaging if possible.
  4. Ship the item(s) to our returns center using a trackable shipping method.
  5. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund or exchange.

Refund Process

Refunds will be issued back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item(s). Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Exchanges

To exchange an item, follow the same process as returns but specify the replacement item you want. If there's a price difference, we'll either charge or refund the difference.

Note: Items must be returned in their original condition. Items showing signs of use, damage, or wear may not be eligible for a full refund.
